    The Dough Launchpad

    Dashboard

    The Dough Dashboard will be the central hub for all Dough launched projects.
    A real-time reward tracker and charting will be available for investors directly from the Dashboard.

    Token Creator*

    The Dough Token Creator allows the creation of a smart contract with as little effort as possible.
    All created tokens are honeypot and rugproof to ensure investors safety.

    Locker

    The Dough Locker will auto lock Liquidity for any token that launches on the Dough Launchpad.
    Ownership and Reward wallets can optionally be locked on the Dough Locker.

    Nft Marketplace

    The Dough NFT Marketplace will allow any project that launched on the Dough Launchpad to create and sell NFTs to investors.
    Trading and galary could be added later on.

    Influencer Hub

    The Dough Influencer Hub helps a token creator source qualitative and legit influencers to promote their project.
    Think Fiverr for crypto influencers.

    Merch Store

    The Dough Merch Store enables token creators to design and sell their unique Merch including a shopfront and shipping.
    Dough Merch is already available for purchase.

    Swap*
    The Dough Swap facilitates investors to easily swap their tokens.Liquidity Farming will be added to the Dough Swap allowing investors to add LP to Dough in return for LP tokens.
